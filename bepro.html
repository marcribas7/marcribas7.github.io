Be Pro
Skills Camp

Founded by Marc Ribas Vilanova

An intensive basketball skills camp in Spain dedicated to unlocking the full potential of young players. Built on professional-level coaching methodology, individual attention, and the belief that greatness is built through deliberate, focused work.

Approach

Training Philosophy

🎯

Individual Focus

Every player gets a personalized development plan. No two athletes are the same — training shouldn't be either. We assess strengths, identify gaps, and build a roadmap for growth.

📹

Video-Driven

Film sessions are a core part of the camp. Players learn to study their own game, understand positioning, and develop basketball IQ through real footage analysis — the same tools used at the professional level.

🔁

Repetition & Detail

Skills are built through high-volume, intentional repetition. We break down every move to its fundamentals — footwork, angles, timing — and rebuild it with precision.

What We Train

Program Areas

On-Court

Ball Handling & Finishing

Advanced dribbling patterns, change of pace, contact finishes, and floater progressions. Building confidence with the ball under pressure.

On-Court

Shooting Mechanics

Form correction, catch-and-shoot rhythm, off-the-dribble shooting, and range extension. Every rep with purpose and feedback.

On-Court

Footwork & Positioning

Offensive and defensive footwork drills, post moves, pivoting, closeout technique, and spatial awareness on both ends of the floor.

Off-Court

Basketball IQ & Film

Video sessions to develop reading ability, decision-making, and understanding of professional-level concepts. Learning to see the game before it happens.

Who It's For

Built for Young Players

Be Pro is designed for youth and development-age basketball players in Spain who are serious about improving their individual game. Whether you're trying to make a competitive team, earn a scholarship, or simply want to reach the next level — this camp is for players who are ready to put in the work.
